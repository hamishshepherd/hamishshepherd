About me

I am a Motion Graphic Designer and editor with 15 years industry experience from Sydney, Australia. I recently moved to London on a 5 year Ancestry Visa. I most recently worked full-time for Network Ten, and then as a freelance Graphic Artist for Ten News First and Studio 10. The role involved both motion and static design. This was for going to air same-day so I am very comfortable under tight deadlines and high pressure environments.

As a freelancer I have worked as a motion designer and editor for numerous clients. I also spent time working in Marketing, with such clients as Hyundai, Galderma and Zoetis.

I've worked in both high-paced television environments with tight deadlines as well as small boutique production houses juggling multiple projects over a long period of time.
